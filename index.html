Vahiny Stations

GYAN - Seamless Configuration & Smart Visualization
GYAN is the ultimate tool for managing and visualizing your pneumatic transport system with ease and efficiency. Built for Linux, it offers a real-time view of your entire system topology and ensures effortless tracking and management—no costly Windows licenses required. Stay ahead with intuitive controls, seamless updates, and robust data security, all tailored to your needs.
- Visualizes the complete system topology
- Real-time status and carrier tracking
- Runs on Linux-based OS
- Simple software updates and data backup/restore
- Local language support and easy mail/push notification configuration
- User, module, and device management
